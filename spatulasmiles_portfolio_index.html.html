The Story

From Classroom
to Camera

Before the channel, there was a classroom. For nearly 9 years, I taught English, History, and Science — learning that communication is everything. That a story told well changes minds.

Spatula Smiles & Lifestyle started in 2020 as a creative outlet. It grew into something more: a real exercise in brand-building, digital strategy, and community. Every subscriber came organically, through consistency and genuine connection.

Today, backed by an MBA and 8+ years of corporate operations experience, I bring both creative instinct and analytical rigour to everything I produce. This channel is my proof of concept.
